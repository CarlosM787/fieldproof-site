TalkEstimate Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

TalkEstimate is a mobile estimating tool for contractors. This policy explains what information the app handles and why. Short version: your data exists to make your estimates work, it is never sold, and there are no ads or cross-app tracking.

Information you provide

  • Your account: your email address (for login codes), your name, business name, phone, and your pricing (cost book, rates, markup).
  • Your customers: names, phone numbers, emails, and project addresses you enter to create estimates. You are responsible for having your customers' permission to enter their details.
  • Voice recordings: jobsite walkthroughs you choose to record.
  • Photos: jobsite photos you choose to attach.
  • Estimates: the line items, prices, and proposals you create.

How it's used

  • Recordings are transcribed and organized into work items so your estimate can be built. Audio is processed by OpenAI for transcription and scope extraction only. AI never sets your prices.
  • Estimates and proposals are stored so you and your customers can view them. Customer proposal links are protected by long random codes.
  • Login emails are delivered through our email provider (Resend).

Where data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (database, file storage, authentication) using encrypted connections. Each contractor's data is isolated — one account can never read another account's information.

What we don't do

  • No selling or renting of your data or your customers' data
  • No advertising and no ad networks
  • No tracking you across other apps or websites
  • No collecting your location

Data deletion

Email us and we will delete your account and all associated data (estimates, recordings, photos, customer records) within 30 days.
